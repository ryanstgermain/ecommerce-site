</Balenciaga Drops $9,000 USD Coat With Seven Built-In-Layers>

Key to the brand’s Fall/Winter 2018 collection, Balenciaga‘s densely-layered coats are finally set to hit store shelves. Despite being a single piece of clothing, the item offers seven distinct layers of different garments, ranging from a hoodie to plaid shirts to fleece jackets, with a drawstring hood allowing for a somewhat adjustable fit. Regardless, the coat’s main purpose, beyond staying warm, is ensuring a massive, imposing silhouette, drawing influence from the layers of clothing worn by skiers as they return from the slopes. Take a closer look at the pre-order on Matchesfashion.com’s website. Recently, Balenciaga has been dropping cold weather knitwear and sweaters ahead of the seasonal shift.

</BAPE & adidas Will Also Drop a Black Camo-Infused UltraBOOST>

A couple of weeks ago, we caught our first look at the upcoming Super Bowl exclusive BAPE x adidas UltraBOOST sneaker, which detailed the streetwear imprint’s signature camo print onto the contemporary runner. With that in mind, Yeezy Mafia hit up Instagram once again, this time to tease the aforementioned sneaker’s tonal black counterpart. The same camo print engulfs the upper portion of the shoe, with the side panel Three Stripes emblem and heel counter breaking up the famed military-themed motif. A matching-hued full-length BOOST midsole placed below rounds out the collaborative model. According to the social media post, the new adidas UltraBOOST will make its debut on February 2, 2019.

</Serena Williams' Off-White™ x Nike Air Max 97 Makes a Surprise Online Release>

Serena Williams‘ Off-White™ x Nike Air Max 97, which was teased by the streetwear label earlier this month, has just made a surprise online release. The silhouette inspired by the tennis superstar first appeared on SNKRS with exclusive access. Now the highly-sought after shoe has dropped once again on SNKRS following Williams’ US Open opening match. For those looking to cop the Off-White™ x Nike Air Max 97 Serena Williams “QUEEN,” head over to SNKRS app’s Behind the Design section to purchase for $190 USD. The Off-White™ x Nike Air Max 97 Serena Williams “QUEEN” is part of a duo pack which also includes the Nike Blazer Mid.
